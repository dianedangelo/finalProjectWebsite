  • Crazy Cat Lady

I don't know what it is about signs. I see an interesting one and I have to have a picture of it. Be it an old drug store sign or a discarded piece of corrugated cardboard that someone was using to ask for money, the sign is not only a beautiful work of art, but a bit of history in our constantly changing world. The human element is intertwined in the stylings of all signage and it's interesting to see different design styles from different eras.
